~ About Us ~

Yan R'esto is a place where classic flavors meet a warm and modern ambiance. Established in 1998, we have become a favorite destination for food lovers seeking an unforgettable dining experience. For over two decades, we have proudly served unique flavors that tantalize the taste buds and bring warmth to every moment with our customers.

We use fresh, high-quality local ingredients to ensure that every dish we serve is not only delicious but also reflects authentic flavors. Our commitment is to maintain the trust of our customers by delivering the best dining experience, from taste to service.
